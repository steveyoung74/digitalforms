2. Our Services

2.1 We can provide advice on, and arrange, all aspects of financial planning such as:

  • (a) individual savings account (ISA) and general investment account (GIA) wrappers;
  • (b) offshore and onshore bonds;

  • (a) personal pensions;
  • (b) stakeholder pensions;
  • (c) self-invested personal pensions (SIPPs);
  • (d) small, self-administered schemes (SSAS);
  • (e) the underlying investment solutions held within (a) to (d) above; and
  • (f) defined benefit pension transfers.

  • (a) life cover (e.g. family income benefit, gift inter vivos cover, shareholder protection, keyman cover, relevant life);
  • (b) critical illness cover; and
  • (c) income protection;

  • (a) annuities; and
  • (b) flexi-access drawdown;

  • (a) gifting advice;

15.2 Our charge for this service varies according to the client profile and objectives and takes into account the complexity of the work required to deliver suitable solutions and the time required for administration and support services.

15.3 We will discuss and agree with you to use one of the following three charging structures as appropriate to your circumstances:

15.3.1 Commission only;

You will not pay us any fee directly and the entirety of our earnings will be received from insurers by way of commission. If you chose this charging structure your regular premium cost for the cover will be higher for the duration of the policy.

If you subsequently cease to pay premiums on the policy, or cancel, surrender or alter the policy within a four year period of the original policy commencing and in consequence we are required to refund the commission that has been paid to us, we reserve the right to recover the same amount via an invoice to you. We will write to you when we do this and say how much we will reclaim.

15.3.2 Fee with commission offset; or

Our fee will be offset by any commission received directly from insurers. Where the commission is lower than the agreed fee you will be responsible for the shortfall.

If you ask us to make arrangements for the commission entitlement from the policy to be offset against our charges to you, then your regular premium cost for the cover will be higher for the duration of the policy.

If you subsequently cease to pay premiums on the policy, or cancel, surrender or alter the policy within a four year period of the original policy commencing and in consequence we are required to refund the commission that has been paid to us and offset against any fees paid by you, we reserve the right to charge you a fee based on the original cost for the advice and service we provided, the number of hours we spent advising you and the complexity of the work arranging the policy. We will write to you when we do this and say how much we will reclaim.

15.3.3 Fee only.

If you decide to settle our fee invoice directly, via a cheque or bank transfer, then the commission entitlement is not taken and this will reduce the cost to you of maintaining the cover for the duration of the policy.

We will agree which of the three charging structures is most appropriate with you before any chargeable activity commences. It can cover the financial review and recommendation as well as the implementation of the recommendation, including liaising with product providers.
